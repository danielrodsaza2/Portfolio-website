Hi I am Daniel

I am Daniel Rodriguez Saza. A Barcelona based industrial and experience designer that practices both as passion and profession. In a nutshell I was born in Colombia, went to college in the U.S and attended the Savannah College of Art and Design pursuing a major in industrial design and a minor in service design and now residing and doing a Master in User Experience at the Barcelona Technology School. I feel inspired by simplicity and the idea that less is more. To create engaging, pleasing and useful products that display beauty, elegance and friendly functionality aiming to make everyday life a bit more interesting and easy.
